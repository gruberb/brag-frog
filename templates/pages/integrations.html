How sync and deletion work

  • Deleting a synced entry hides it permanently from normal syncs — it won't come back.
  • Hard Resync restores all previously deleted entries for that service and re-fetches everything. Entries that no longer match your sync settings are removed, but entries you've edited (key result assignments, teams, collaborators) are always preserved.
  • Manual entries are permanently deleted immediately.
