The 10x Engineer Playbook

Getting things done = breakdown + communication. Break work down immediately. Never say "we're behind" — frame delays as tradeoffs.

Boundaries fuel performance. Protect your non-negotiable blocks. Having less time forces more efficient use of it.

Relationships > raw skill at senior+ levels. Talk to engineers on other teams before their managers. Approach from the customer's perspective. Give and take.

Low ego + genuine curiosity is a superpower. Treat all engineers as peers. Proactively schedule 1:1s with PMs and designers.

Ownership separates good from great. Own tasks end-to-end. Your manager shouldn't need to check in.
